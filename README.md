# Adidas-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
📊 Adidas Sales Dashboard – Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Adidas Sales Performance Dashboard designed to deliver comprehensive insights into regional performance, product trends, retailer contributions, and sales channel effectiveness. The dashboard enables dynamic exploration of sales data to support strategic and data-driven decision-making.

🔎 Dashboard Features
1️⃣ Interactive Filtering System

The dashboard includes advanced slicers that allow users to filter and analyze data across multiple dimensions:

Region

Retailer

Product Category

City

State

Sales Method (In-store, Online, Outlet)

These controls provide real-time interactivity, enabling users to drill down into specific segments and customize the analysis based on business needs.

2️⃣ Regional Profit Analysis

A regional distribution visualization highlights how profitability varies across different geographic areas. This view helps identify high-performing regions and uncover areas with growth potential.

3️⃣ Product Performance & Pareto Analysis

The dashboard incorporates a Pareto (80/20) analysis to demonstrate how a limited number of product categories contribute to the majority of total sales. This visualization supports strategic focus on top-performing product segments while identifying opportunities for optimization.

4️⃣ Executive KPI Summary

Prominent KPI cards provide a clear executive overview of overall performance, including:

Total sales performance

Highest-performing retailer

These indicators offer immediate insight into business health at a glance.

5️⃣ Retailer Performance Comparison

A comparative bar chart evaluates sales performance across key retail partners. This section enables stakeholders to assess retailer contribution, benchmark performance, and support partnership strategy decisions.

6️⃣ Sales Channel Distribution

A sales method breakdown illustrates the performance of different distribution channels, including in-store, online, and outlet sales. This analysis highlights channel effectiveness and evolving consumer purchasing behavior.

7️⃣ Quarterly Sales Trend Analysis

A time-series visualization tracks sales performance across multiple quarters and years. This trend analysis helps identify growth patterns, seasonal fluctuations, and overall business momentum.

🎯 Key Insights

Clear visibility into regional profitability distribution

Identification of top-performing product categories

Recognition of leading retail partners

Strong understanding of sales channel performance

Improved ability to track and interpret sales trends over time

🛠️ Project Objective

This dashboard was developed to:

Provide a centralized and interactive sales performance overview

Support executive-level reporting and strategic planning

Enable data-driven decision-making

Apply business intelligence principles for performance optimization
